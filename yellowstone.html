🎯 Trip Activities — Cool Things We're Doing
🌅⭐ Tetons Sunrise · Oxbow BendDay 4
4:30am alarm. 15 min from Jackson. Snake River reflection of Mount Moran with alpenglow. The trip's signature moment.
🐴⭐ Family Horseback RideDay 7
1-hour ride at Canyon Corral. Classic Yellowstone backcountry trail. ~$80–100/person.
🛶⭐ Bridge Bay Lake AfternoonDay 7
Marina is a walk from camp. Kayak the bay, take the Lake Queen cruise, or guided fishing.
🥾⭐ Fairy Falls + Grand PrismaticDay 6
5-mile roundtrip hike. Hits the Grand Prismatic Overlook AND a 197-ft waterfall. The single best Yellowstone hike.
♨️⭐ Old Faithful GeyserDay 6
Erupts every ~90 min. Check predicted times at Visitor Center. Walk Geyser Hill Loop.
💦⭐ Artist Point + Lower FallsDay 7
308-ft waterfall in the Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone — twice the height of Niagara.
🏔️⭐ Togwotee Pass RevealDay 3
Last 90 minutes of the drive into Jackson. The entire Teton range erupts with no foothills.
⛵Jenny Lake Boat + Hidden FallsDay 4
$18/person RT shuttle boat. Short hike to Hidden Falls (100 ft) and Inspiration Point.
🏊String Lake SwimDay 4
Shallow, calm, crystal clear. Swimmable in July with Tetons reflected in the water.
🦬Hayden Valley WildlifeDay 7
Bison herds in the hundreds. Possibly grizzlies, wolves, bald eagles. Watch for spotting-scope clusters.
🎸Red Rocks SunsetDay 1
Walk the natural amphitheater carved into 300-ft sandstone. Trading Post Trail loop.
⚒️School of Mines Geology MuseumDay 1
FREE. Dinosaur fossils, mineral specimens. ~45 min. Walk the Golden campus after.
🎓CU Boulder Campus VisitDay 2
Noah's college visit. Walk Farrand Field + Memorial Center + Pearl Street Mall next door.
⛺Bridge Bay CampingDays 5-7
12×12 family site, 2 tents, on Yellowstone Lake's shore. Campfires, 🌌 stargazing, ranger programs.
♨️West Thumb Geyser BasinDay 5
Geothermal features right on the lakeshore. Fishing Cone bubbles in the lake itself.
🏞️North Fork Shoshone CanyonDay 8
"50 most beautiful miles in America" (T. Roosevelt). Between East Entrance and Cody.
🏛️Buffalo Bill Center (optional)Day 8
5 museums in one in Cody. Plains Indian, Firearms, Western Art, Natural History, Buffalo Bill.
🌌Stargazing at Bridge BayDays 5-7
Milky Way visible on clear nights. Dark sky territory. Watch for early Perseids.
